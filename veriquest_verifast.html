What is VeriQuest?

Introducing VeriQuest — Your CLI Verification AI

VeriQuest is the industry-first CLI AI Agent built specifically for ASIC/FPGA Design Verification teams. It sits inside your terminal and understands your chip.

Developed by VeriFast Technologies' Systems & Software team, VeriQuest transforms raw simulation data — logs, RTL, waveforms — into structured insights, root causes, and instant Jira tickets.

"Stop Searching, Start Fixing." — VeriQuest puts you in control of every failure from the first keystroke.

🔮 Phase 2 — Future Feature

Spec-Integrated Debugging (Spec-RAG)

The AI reads your architectural specification so you don't have to.

📄
What it does

Connects simulation failures directly to your architectural specification documents (PDFs) using Retrieval-Augmented Generation.

🤖
How it works

The AI reads the spec manual for you — cross-referencing "expected behavior" from the PDF with "actual behavior" observed in simulation logs.

🎯
Value Delivered

Instantly surfaces spec vs. silicon mismatches, saving engineers hours of manual spec-diving per debug session.

🛣️

Phase 2 Roadmap: Spec-RAG is the next evolution of VeriQuest. While Phase 1 connects you to RTL + logs, Phase 2 adds the specification layer — making VeriQuest a complete, closed-loop DV intelligence platform.
